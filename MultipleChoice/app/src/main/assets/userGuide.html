User Guide

Hello, thanks a lot for downloading this app. Here's a quick How-To to get you started.

This is the Start screen. Click "Start Quiz"

1. End Quiz Now button. Press this button to stop the game before finishing all 50 questions of the quiz if you dont feel like finishing the whole quiz, or you're just checking in for your daily dose of facts.

2. Question Counter: Shows how far away you are from finishing the quiz.

3. Shows the current top score, to motivate you.

4. Shows your score.

Everytime you get a question right, the question card flashes green and your score goes up by 1!

Everytime you get a question wrong, the question card flashes red and you get a toast to tell you what the right answer was.

This is the end game screen. You come here after finishing the quiz or, clicking the "End quiz now button" to exit halfway. The top score and your score is visible here. WARNING: DO NOT press the back button on your phone to end the quiz halfway as you will end up in the start screen and won't be able to VIEW the scores.
